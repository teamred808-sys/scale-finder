Privacy Policy

App: Scale Finder
Operator: TeamRed808 (RedBlood Music)
Last updated: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Scale Finder (“the App”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) accesses, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the App.

Key point: Voice and instrument recordings used for scale and key detection are analyzed locally on your device. We do not upload your audio recordings to our servers for analysis.

1. Information We Access and Handle

A. Microphone and audio input

If you use features such as Detect from Voice or Detect from Instrument, the App requests access to your device’s microphone so it can record audio you choose to provide.

  • We access microphone audio only when you start a recording or use an audio-detection feature.
  • Audio analysis for pitch, key, and scale detection is intended to run on-device.
  • We do not upload your raw voice or instrument recordings to our servers for analysis.

B. App content and settings stored on your device

The App may store information locally on your device so that core features work properly and your experience is preserved.

  • Saved favorites
  • Recent searches or analyses
  • App preferences and settings
  • Cached analysis results needed for performance or usability

This information is generally stored locally on your device unless a feature clearly states otherwise.

C. Advertising-related data

The free version of the App may display ads through third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. Those providers may collect or receive certain information automatically in order to serve, measure, secure, or improve ads.

  • Device or advertising identifiers
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • App and device interaction data related to ads
  • Diagnostic, performance, and fraud-prevention signals

Collection and use of ad-related data by third-party providers is governed by their own privacy policies and settings.

D. Subscription and payment information

If you choose a premium subscription, purchases are processed by your app marketplace or payment platform, such as Google Play. We do not directly collect or store your full payment card details.

We may receive limited subscription-related information needed to confirm your premium status, such as purchase status, plan type, renewal state, or transaction identifiers.

E. Support communications

If you contact us directly, we may receive the information you include in your message, such as your email address and the contents of your request.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide audio-based key and scale detection features
  • Save your preferences, favorites, and recent activity on your device
  • Show ads in the free version of the App
  • Confirm premium subscription status and remove ads for eligible users
  • Maintain app security, reliability, and basic troubleshooting
  • Respond to support requests or legal obligations

3. When We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information in the following situations:

  • Advertising providers: if you use the free version with ads, ad partners such as Google AdMob may receive device and ad-related data as described above.
  • Billing providers: app marketplaces such as Google Play may process payment and subscription information under their own terms and privacy policies.
  • Legal reasons: if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, or security.
  • Business transfers: if the App or related assets are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable law.

4. On-Device Audio Analysis

Scale Finder is designed so that recorded voice or instrument audio used for detection is processed on-device for analysis. In normal operation, we do not upload your raw audio recordings to our servers for pitch, key, or scale analysis.

If we ever introduce a cloud-based analysis feature in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and the relevant in-app disclosures before that feature is used.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local app data: favorites, recent results, and settings stored on your device remain there until you delete them, clear app data, or uninstall the App.
  • Support messages: if you contact us, we may keep those communications for as long as reasonably necessary to respond, maintain records, and meet legal obligations.
  • Third-party data: data processed by Google AdMob, Google Play, or other third-party providers is subject to those providers’ own retention policies.

If you want locally stored app data removed, you can delete it in-app if the feature is available, clear the App’s storage in your device settings, or uninstall the App.

6. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information associated with the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children’s Privacy

Scale Finder is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law to provide valid consent on their own, unless explicitly stated otherwise in the App listing or product experience. If you believe a child has provided us information in a way that is not appropriate, please contact us.

8. International Processing

Third-party providers such as Google AdMob or Google Play may process information in countries other than your own, subject to their own terms, privacy practices, and applicable law.

9. Your Choices

  • You can manage app permissions through your device settings.
  • You can choose a premium subscription to remove ads, if available.
  • You may be able to adjust ad personalization or consent settings where required by law and supported by the ad provider.
